# Escalation Matrix

## Overview
This matrix dictates who handles high-severity or sensitive issues.

## Legal Threats
- **Who Handles:** Legal Team (`legal-team@senai.io`)
- **Action:** Any mention of "sue", "lawyer", "legal action", or "cease and desist" must be immediately escalated to Legal. Support agents should hold on replying.

## Security Incidents
- **Who Handles:** Security Operations (`secops@senai.io`)
- **Action:** Ransomware, unauthorized access, or data breaches must be flagged as Critical and routed to SecOps.

## PR Crises
- **Who Handles:** PR & Comms Team (`pr@senai.io`)
- **Action:** If a user with high reach threatens public reviews (G2, Trustpilot, Twitter) or a reporter inquires, escalate to PR.

## VIP Churns
- **Who Handles:** Customer Success Managers (CSM)
- **Action:** If a VIP (Enterprise) customer shows a high churn risk or requests cancellation, route to their dedicated CSM immediately.

## GDPR Requests
- **Who Handles:** Privacy Officer / Compliance Team
- **Action:** Formal GDPR requests (e.g., Article 20, Right to be Forgotten) must be routed to Compliance and fulfilled within the 30-day statutory window.
