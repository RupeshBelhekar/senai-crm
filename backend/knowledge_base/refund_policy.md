# Refund Policy

## Standard Refund Window
- No refunds will be provided after 14 days from the initial purchase date or renewal date.

## Exception Process
- Exceptions may be granted for extended service outages or billing errors. These must be approved by a Billing Manager.

## Credits vs. Refunds
- We generally prefer issuing account credits for future use rather than direct cash refunds. Credits never expire. Direct refunds to the original payment method take 5-10 business days.

## Churn Retention Playbook
- If a customer requests a refund or cancellation due to dissatisfaction or pricing, support agents must offer a "retention discount" of 20% off for the next 3 months before processing the cancellation.
- If the customer mentions a competitor, attempt to gather feedback and escalate to the product team, offering a free consultation.
