# SLA Policy

## Uptime Commitment
We guarantee a 99.9% uptime Service Level Agreement (SLA) for our core APIs and dashboard.

## Incident Response Times
- **P0 (Critical Outage):** 15 minutes
- **P1 (High Impact):** 1 hour
- **P2 (Medium Impact):** 4 hours
- **P3 (Low Impact):** 24 business hours

## Credit Calculation Formula
If uptime falls below 99.9% in a given month, customers are eligible for service credits:
- 99.0% - 99.89%: 10% credit of monthly fee
- < 99.0%: 30% credit of monthly fee

## RCA Delivery SLA
For all P0 incidents, a formal Root Cause Analysis (RCA) document will be delivered to affected customers within 24 hours of incident resolution.
