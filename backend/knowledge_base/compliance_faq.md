# Compliance FAQ

## HIPAA BAA Availability
- We can sign a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) for HIPAA compliance, but this is ONLY available for customers on the Enterprise tier.

## GDPR DPA Process
- We provide a standard Data Processing Agreement (DPA) for GDPR compliance. This can be signed digitally via our trust center.
- For Article 20 data portability requests, we must respond and provide the user's data within 30 days of the request.

## SOC 2 Type II Status
- We are SOC 2 Type II certified. The full report is available under NDA.

## Data Residency Options
- Customers can choose to host their data in US (N. Virginia), EU (Frankfurt), or APAC (Tokyo). This selection must be made during account provisioning.
