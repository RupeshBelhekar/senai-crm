# Pricing Policy

## Overview
This document outlines our pricing structure and policies for all SenAI products.

## Pricing Tiers
- **Starter:** $49/month. Includes 1 seat, up to 1,000 processed emails, standard support.
- **Standard:** $149/month. Includes 5 seats, up to 10,000 processed emails, priority support.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing. Unlimited seats and emails. Requires a custom contract.

## Special Discounts
- **Non-Profit Discount:** We offer a 30% discount on the Standard tier for verified non-profit organizations.

## Billing Rules
- **Pro-rata Billing:** Upgrades in the middle of a billing cycle will be charged on a pro-rata basis for the remainder of the current cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Custom Pricing
- Enterprise custom pricing involves negotiated volume discounts and custom SLAs. Contact sales for details.
